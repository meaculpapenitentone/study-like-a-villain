## Full Description

Study Like a Villain is a focused study planner designed for students who want a simple, structured way to organize their study sessions.

Create your subject list, set a priority level for each subject, choose your total study time, and generate a personalized study cycle. The app automatically organizes your blocks based on priority, helping you spend more time on the subjects that matter most.

Each study cycle includes a clean timer, progress tracking, saved cycles, multilingual support, and a focused interface built to keep you moving.

Features:
- Create custom study subjects
- Set priority levels: low, medium, or high
- Generate weighted study cycles
- Choose total study time and block duration
- Use a built-in study timer
- Pause and reorganize your current or future blocks
- Save and resume study cycles
- Switch between multiple languages
- Enjoy rotating philosophical quotes for extra motivation

Whether you are preparing for exams, organizing a school semester, studying for a certification, or building a daily study routine, Study Like a Villain helps you plan with clarity and study with focus.
